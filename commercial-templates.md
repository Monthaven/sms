## üìã **Best Practices**

1. **Keep initial messages under 160 characters** when possible
2. **Always include your name** for credibility
3. **Reference specific property details** to show you've done research  
4. **Create urgency** with market timing or competition
5. **Offer value** (market insights, comparables) not just purchase interest
6. **Use professional language** appropriate for commercial transactions
7. **Test templates** with A/B splits to optimize performance
8. **Track response rates** by template and property type

## üéØ **Property-Specific Customizations**

### High-Value Properties ($5M+)
- More formal tone
- Longer relationship-building sequences  
- Emphasis on discretion and professionalism
- Reference to institutional backing

### Distressed Properties
- Faster timeline urgency
- Solution-focused messaging
- Reference to quick closings
- Emphasis on "as-is" purchases

### Owner-Occupied Properties  
- Acknowledge business operations
- Offer leaseback options
- Longer decision timelines
- More relationship-focused approach
