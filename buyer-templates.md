## üèÜ **HIGH-IMPACT BUYER PHRASES**

### Power Phrases to Include:
- **"Cash buyer"** - Eliminates financing risk
- **"30-day close"** - Speed advantage  
- **"No contingencies"** - Deal certainty
- **"Just closed on..."** - Proof of execution
- **"Direct from owner"** - No broker hassles
- **"All cash, fast close"** - Double benefit
- **"I move quickly"** - Urgency creator
- **"Keep the commission"** - Money in their pocket
- **"Proven track record"** - Builds confidence
- **"Deploying capital"** - Shows serious intent

### Credibility Builders:
- Reference recent acquisitions
- Mention specific $ amounts available
- Reference local market knowledge  
- Show understanding of their property type
- Demonstrate speed of execution
- Offer proof of funds upfront
